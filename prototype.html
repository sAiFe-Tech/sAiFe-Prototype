Common questions

What if my mum doesn't have a smartphone?
She doesn't need one. Records about her can sit on the household plan, kept by you. The Family Pack she might need is delivered as a calm, printable document — no app required.
Can I start solo and upgrade later?
Yes. Everything you've built on the £6.99 plan moves with you. Nothing has to be re-entered. The upgrade is one tap.
Can my partner edit too, or just see?
On the household plan, both of you can edit — and you control which records they can see, edit, or release. The defaults are sensible; you can tighten them at any point.
What happens if I cancel?
Your records are exported cleanly as PDFs and a structured JSON file before the account closes. Nothing is held hostage.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — 30 days, no card needed to start. The Family Pack release feature is the only thing kept behind the paid plan.
Annual or monthly?
Either. Annual saves around 14% on both plans. Switch from one to the other at any renewal.
More than five people in our household?
Get in touch. We'll add a small per-person fee rather than push you onto something more complex than you need.
